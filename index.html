BMI Calculator

BMI Calculator is a tool that helps you assess how your weight-height relation can affect your health. BMI result is the division of the weight by squared height. The score shows if the person is underweight, normal, overweight or obese. The tool should not be used by people under 18 years old, pregnant or lactating women or a person with an eating disorder.
